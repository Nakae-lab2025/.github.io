2nd Digital Brain Workshop

Enhancing communication with students and young researchers

Where mathematics, brain science, and fresh ideas collide
Leave the city lights behind and spend three days on a mountain ridge where minds meet nature. Join a hand-picked team of young Japanese and Korean researchers, engage in face-to-face discussions, and sprint toward innovative cross-border collaborations. Surrounded by hot-spring steam and star-filled skies, you'll trade lecture notes for live-fire discussion, guided by internationally renowned mentors.

Team Structure

Each team consists of:

  • 1 Japanese Brain Scientist
  • 1 Japanese Mathematician
  • 1 Korean Scientist
  • 2-3 additional members
  • 1 tutor (organizer or invited presenter)
