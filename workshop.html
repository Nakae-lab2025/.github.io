2nd Digital Brain Workshop

Enhancing communication with students and young researchers

Joint workshop of Korean Society for Computational Neuroscience

Leave the city lights behind and spend three days on a mountain ridge where mathematics, brain science, and fresh ideas collide. At the 2nd Digital Brain Workshop you will join a hand-picked team of young Japanese and Korean researchers, debate face-to-face in English, and sprint toward a 10-minute joint proposal that could spark the next cross-border collaboration. Surrounded by hot-spring steam and star-filled skies, you'll trade lecture notes for live-fire discussion, guided by internationally renowned mentors.
